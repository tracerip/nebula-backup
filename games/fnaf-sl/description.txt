Mouse - Move between stations / cameras.
Left Click - Shock / Oxygen / Hacks / Interact.
Hold Click - Maintain flashlight / defenses.
WASD - Character movement (minigames).
Space - Quick shock/jump.
Escape - Menu / Quit.
