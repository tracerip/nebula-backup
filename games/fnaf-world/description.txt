# Description
[FNaF World](https://steamdb.info/app/427920/) ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FNaF_World)) is a quirky chibi-style RPG adventure starring over 48 recruitable animatronics exploring a colorful overworld. Assemble parties, battle enemies with unique attacks and chips, unlock characters like Desk Man guides, and conquer challenging areas, bosses, and modes like Fixed Party or Adventure for hours of strategic fun and FNaF lore Easter eggs.

If you're looking for a different gaming experience, try [FNaF Pizza Simulator](play?game=fnaf-ps) or [FNaF Ultimate Custom Night](play?game=fnaf-ucn).

# Controls
Arrow Keys / WASD - Move characters / Navigate menus.
Mouse - Select attacks / chips / party members.
Left Click - Confirm actions / battles.
Space - Quick menu / skip.
1-9 - Party slots (combat).
Escape - Menu / Save.
