# Description
[FNaF 4: Halloween Edition](https://store.steampowered.com/app/388090/Five_Nights_at_Freddys_4/) ([FNaF Wiki](https://freddy-fazbears-pizza.fandom.com/wiki/Five_Nights_at_Freddy%27s_4:_Halloween_Edition)) is the spine-chilling Halloween-themed edition of FNaF 4, swapping in horrifying new animatronics like Jack-O-Chica, Nightmare Mangle, Nightmarionne, and Nightmare Balloon Boy. Defend the child's bedroom by listening for breaths, flashing the flashlight on doors and closets, and winding the music box amid intensified scares, Halloween atmosphere, and brutal challenge modes.

If you're looking for a different gaming experience, try [FNaF Ultimate Custom Night](play?game=fnaf-ucn) or [FNaF World](play?game=fnaf-world).

# Controls
Mouse - Shine flashlight (door / closet).
Left Click - Flashlight on / off.
Hold Click - Continuous light.
